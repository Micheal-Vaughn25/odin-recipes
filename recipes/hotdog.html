Hotdog

Description

Swooning over the consummate hot dog in that centerfold-worthy photo above?
Yeah, so are we. So get thee a bun, nestle a frankfurter within it,
and squeeze on a zigzag of yellow mustard and a squirt of sweet pickle
relish or whatever other condiments you fancy after heeding the words of advice below.


Ingredients

  • Hotdogs
  • Hotdog Buns
  • Relish
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Open the hotdog bun
  2. Place hotdog in bun
  3. Add relish to hotdog
  4. Add mustard to hotdog
