Hamburger

Description

Need a basic burger recipe that you can whip up in no time?
We have the perfect one. With just 5 ingredients and 15 minutes of cooking time,
this flavorful recipe is ready to be shared, topped, and sauced in any way you like!


Ingredients

  • Hamburger Meat
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Cheese
  • Pickles
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Grill the hamburger meat
  2. Spread mustard,ketchup, or mayo on hamburger bun
  3. Place hamburger patty on bun
  4. Place cheese on hamburger patty
  5. Place lettuce on top of cheese
  6. Place pickles on top of lettuce
